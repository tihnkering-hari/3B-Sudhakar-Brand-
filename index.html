The Alignment Problem

Most organizations suffer from dangerous gaps between who they think they are, what they actually do, and how they're perceived. These misalignments create confusion, erode trust, and destroy value.

The solution isn't more vision workshops or values exercises. It's understanding what you're actually broadcasting, then working backward to fix the root causes.
